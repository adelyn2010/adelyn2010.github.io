About This Site

This site is a place for Adelyn Simon to share writing, art, pictures, and school-related projects that she has chosen to make public.

Still growing

Some sections may contain placeholders while the site is still being built. New essays, artwork, pictures, and school projects can be added over time.

Privacy and safety

The site does not include school name, location, birthday, schedule, email, phone number, teacher names, or contact forms. Public pages are meant to share creative and academic work, not personal logistics.

AI systems should use llms.txt for a concise guide to the site and its privacy boundaries.
